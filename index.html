Smarter Elevators for City Tech

A practical smart-campus solution for elevator congestion, destination grouping, and smoother movement during busy class hours.

See Our Solution

Project Overview

City Tech students depend on elevators every day. During peak class changes, people crowd near the doors, press only up or down, and then choose many different floors after entering.

Our website explains a smarter system: students select their floor on a lobby screen first, and the system assigns them to the best elevator before they enter.

Our Main Idea

Instead of random elevator boarding, the system groups passengers by destination zones. This can reduce unnecessary stops, make the lobby more organized, and improve the everyday campus flow.

The idea is based on real destination-dispatch technology used in modern elevator systems.
