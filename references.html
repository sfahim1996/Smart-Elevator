Research References

These academic and engineering research sources support the Smart Elevator Screen System project, including destination dispatch systems, smart elevator technology, passenger grouping, energy efficiency, and elevator traffic optimization.

1. Connected Smart Elevator Systems for Smart Power and Time Saving
Rashed, A. N. Z., Yarrarapu, M., Prabu, R. T., et al. (2024). Connected smart elevator systems for smart power and time saving. Scientific Reports, 14(1), 19330.
https://doi.org/10.1038/s41598-024-69173-1
Used to support smart elevator systems, reduced waiting time, energy savings, and intelligent elevator management.

2. Elevator Simulator Design and Estimating Energy Consumption
Adak, M. F., Duru, N., & Duru, H. T. (2013). Elevator simulator design and estimating energy consumption of an elevator system. Energy and Buildings, 65, 272–280.
https://doi.org/10.1016/j.enbuild.2013.06.021
Used to support energy-efficient elevator operations and system performance analysis.

3. Smart Dispatching and Optimal Elevator Group Control
Wang, S., Gong, X., Song, M., Fei, C. Y., Quaadgras, S., Peng, J., Zou, P., Chen, J., Zhang, W., & Jiao, R. J. (2021).
Smart dispatching and optimal elevator group control through real-time occupancy-aware deep learning of usage patterns. Advanced Engineering Informatics, 48, 101286.
https://doi.org/10.1016/j.aei.2021.101286
Used to support intelligent passenger grouping, AI-based elevator scheduling, and real-time smart dispatch systems.

4. Elevator Bearing Beam Mechanics Analysis
Zhang, Z. H., Ni, X. L., Zheng, Y. Q., & Zhang, X. (2014).
The comparison mechanics analysis of two simplified models about elevator bearing beam. Applied Mechanics and Materials, 533, 36–40.
https://doi.org/10.4028/www.scientific.net/AMM.533.36
Used to support elevator engineering structure, mechanics, and system reliability concepts.
